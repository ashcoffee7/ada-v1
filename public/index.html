Being a female in America is not easy. It becomes even worse when we don't have access to critical healthcare resources we need - birth control, STD testing, sexual violence reporting, and more. Ada was born out of a need to help women and trans individuals find the closest low-cost women and gender-affirming healthcare resources to them, as soon as possible. Because everyone deserves free, accessible healthcare.
